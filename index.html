About

Idan Amedi was born at 1988 in Pisgat Ze'ev, Jerusalem. He is an israeli singer, writer, composer and actor.
He began his singing career in an audition on "Star Born", when he performed with the song "Pain of Warriors" he wrote and composed himself about his experiences as a warrior during his military service. With the end of that "Star Born" season in 2010, the song "Pain of Warriors" came out as his first single on the radio stations, reaching first place on the playlists in Israel.
Until now Idan release four successful albums:
1.Idan Amedi(עידן עמדי)
2.Bazman A'aharon(בזמן האחרון)
3.Rachinoo Lihiyot(רצינו להיות)
4.Helek Mehazman(חלק מהזמן).
Some of his successful songs are: Nigmar(נגמר),Ani Roche(אני רוצה),Helek Mehazman(חלק מהזמן) etc.
At 2017, he began his acting career in the second season of Pauda, when he acting Sagi, joins to the special military unit.

Three differences between web1, web2 and web3
1. Web1 Supports read-only, web2 supports reading and writing and web3 in addition to web2, it supports both Portable and Personal computers.
2. Web1 was for Companies only, web2 was mainly for Communities and web3 in addition to web2, for individuals.
3. Web1 contains only forms on the web, web2 contains also web apps, and web3 contains smart apps.

For example: The query "Information about Israel"
Web1 will display all existing links in an orderly manner, and the user will have to search for the relevant links himself. Web2 uses a search engine (app) to find the relevant pages. This version will show only the specific pages that match with the query. Web3 uses a smart search engine (smart app) to find the relevant pages. This version will show the pages that mach with the query and pages with common topics.
